Solve the Puzzle

Attempt to solve the puzzle: If the guess is correct, your Total Caps will be increased by your Round Caps.

Spin the Wheel

Spin the wheel: When the wheel is spun there are three types of outcomes, a Cap amount to be added to your Round Caps, a bankruptcy amount which will cause the Round Caps to be set to 0, or a Lose a Turn.

Buy a Vowel for 100 Caps. The vowel will be entered into the board to further the puzzle
